About Advocates for Rare Diseases

The Cause

Rare diseases affect approximately one in ten people. Despite this collective rarity, these diseases affect millions globally. A lack of understanding presents challenges in diagnosis, treatment, and research.

The Solution

While this problem impacts millions, individuals facing these challenges often do so in isolation because of limited awareness or support. Advocating for rare diseases involves raising awareness, fostering collaboration, and advocating for patient support initiatives.

Our Mission

Empower students to become catalysts for change by uniting them in the fight against rare diseases. Through education, collaboration, and advocacy, we aim to raise awareness, foster understanding, and provide support for those affected, ensuring that no one faces these challenges alone.
